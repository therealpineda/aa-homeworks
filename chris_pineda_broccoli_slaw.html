Broccoli Cole Slaw

Recipe by Paula Deen

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description

Broccoli slaw is a variation of traditional coleslaw with shredded raw broccoli stalks substituted for cabbage. This particular version uses toasted ramen noodles, almonds, and sunflower seeds, with brown sugar and vinegar in the dressing to make for a nice light (tasting!) upgrade to that boring old coleslaw.

Ingredients

    Salad

  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 2 (3 oz) bags ramen noodle soup in Oriental (other flavors work too)
  • 1/4 cup slivered almonds
  • 2 (12 oz) bags broccoli cole slaw mix
  • 1/4 cup sunflower seeds
  • chopped green onions, for garnish

    • Dressing

  • 3/4 cup canola oil
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar or white sugar
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1 ramen noodle seasoning packet

Directions

  1. Melt butter in a large skillet over low/medium heat
  2. Put ramen noodles in a bag and crush them with a rolling pin
  3. Add noodles and almonds to skillet and saute, stirring occasionally (keep temperature at low/medium heat) until golden brown
  4. Meanwhile, whisk together: canola oil, sugar, apple cider vinegar, and ramen noodle seasoning packet in a small bowl
  5. Place shredded broccoli into large bowl and toss with the noodles, almonds, and sunflower seeds
  6. Pour dressing over salad and toss to coat
  7. Garnish with chopped green onions
